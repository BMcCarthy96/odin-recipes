Chicken Casserole

Chicken casserole is a delicious dish to make for your family! This dish is creamy, savory, and absolutely a dish you want on your dinner table!


How to Make Chicken Casserole:

  1. Boil the pasta, drain.

  2. Cook chicken and shred.

  3. Mix shredded chicken with soup, cover with cheese.

  4. Top with breadcrumbs.

  5. Bake and prepare to thoroughly enjoy!

Ingredients:

  • 3 cups shredded cooked chicken
  • 2 cans (10.5 ounces each) condensed cream of chicken soup
  • 2 cups shredded sharp Cheddar cheese (8 ounces)
  • 3 cups Progresso plain panko crispy bread crumbs
  • 6 tablespoons butter, melted

Step-by-Step Guide

  1. Heat oven to 350Â°F.

  2. Place chicken evenly in bottom of ungreased 13x9-inch (3-quart) glass baking dish. Spoon and spread soup evenly over chicken; sprinkle with cheese.

  3. In medium bowl, stir bread crumbs and melted butter; sprinkle over cheese. Bake 30 to 35 minutes or until cheese is melted and bread crumbs are golden brown and thoroughly heated.
